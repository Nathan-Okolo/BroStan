About Me

I work with Spotter Construction Ltd and Spotter Homes as the CEO/MD and also the Chairman of Globe Link Event & Entertainment and Spotter Magazine.

I am a real estate developer and a contractor with over ten years experience. I am a civil Engineer from Enugu State University of Science and Technology and have been awarded with so may honors due to my contributions in the field of engineering. I also stand in as the chief site engineer of Spotter Construction Company Ltd with a work experience of 22 years. My passion for arts and design made me work on constructions while in school. My hunger for creation of beautiful edifices has increased my appetite to work on structures that are out of the box. I started very small, By building residential houses for family and friends before it evolved into a multi-billion naira company because of my outstanding works. I am the brain and sole developer of Spotter Lifestyle Estate phase 1 and 2 and Skylar Estate in Enugu state and Many more. I am a Catholic knight of Saint John International and the founder of Shelter-Dem Foundation, a foundation that has built houses for over 40 indigent people in Nigeria.
